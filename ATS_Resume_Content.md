### **Indian Stock Market AI Decision & Wealth Management Platform**
**Technologies:** Python, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, LightGBM, SHAP, SQLite, Pandas, NumPy, textblob, ta (Technical Analysis), Flask
* **Predictive Modeling:** Built and evaluated multiple machine learning classifiers (Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM, Logistic Regression) to predict stock movement trends for major NSE stocks, achieving optimal training classification accuracy.
* **Feature Engineering:** Developed a multi-factor pipeline to engineer technical, fundamental, and sentiment features; calculated indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands) using the `ta` library, and integrated dynamic trailing P/E and P/B valuation metrics.
* **Explainable AI (XAI):** Implemented SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) using TreeExplainer and KernelExplainer to visualize model feature importances, generating real-time natural language explanations for model decisions to ensure transparency.
* **Sentiment Analysis Pipeline:** Engineered an NLP news scraper using Yahoo Finance and Google News RSS feeds to parse financial headlines, using TextBlob to extract polarity scores and enrich feature inputs.
* **Data Storage & Ingestion:** Architected a robust data store utilizing SQLite, supporting historical daily market data, real-time live price injection for intraday tracking, and automated concurrent multi-threading updates.
