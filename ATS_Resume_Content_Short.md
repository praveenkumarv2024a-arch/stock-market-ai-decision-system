**Indian Stock Market AI Decision & Recommendation System**
**Technologies:** Python, Flask, SQLite, Machine Learning, SHAP, Natural Language Processing (NLP)

* Developed machine learning models (XGBoost, LightGBM, Random Forest) utilizing technical indicators (RSI, MACD), news sentiment analysis (NLP), and valuation metrics to predict stock movements.
* Integrated the models with an interactive Flask web dashboard featuring real-time charts, SHAP explainable AI explanations, and a simulated paper trading engine.
